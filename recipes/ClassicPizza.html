Classic Pizza

A classic Italian recipe for a simple yet delicious pizza.

Details

  • Prep: 2 hrs
  • Cook: 15 mins
  • Total: 2 hrs 15 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 large ball fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves

Directions

  1. Combine flour, water, salt, and yeast in a mixing bowl. Knead until smooth.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rise at room temperature for 1 to 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to its maximum temperature. If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven.
  4. Divide dough into 4 pieces and shape each into a pizza base.
  5. Spread a layer of crushed tomatoes over each pizza base. Slice the mozzarella and arrange it on top.
  6. Bake each pizza in the oven for about 15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with a drizzle of olive oil and a few fresh basil leaves.
