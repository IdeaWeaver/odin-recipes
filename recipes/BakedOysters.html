Baked Oysters

A simple and delicious grilled oysters recipe, perfect for a quick and tasty meal.

Details

  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: 25 mins
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 24 fresh oysters
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat grill for medium heat.
  2. Rinse oysters and place on the preheated grill.
  3. Combine garlic, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, black pepper, oregano, melted butter, parsley, olive oil, and hot pepper sauce in a small bowl.
  4. Spoon 1 to 2 teaspoons of the cheese mixture onto each oyster.
  5. Grill until oysters are opaque and cheese is melted, 7 to 10 minutes.
